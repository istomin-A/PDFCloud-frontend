Frequently asked questions

What is PDF and how is it different from other formats?

PDF (Portable Document Format) is a universal file format designed for easy viewing and sharing of documents. It preserves the original formatting of text, images, and styles on any device and operating system.

How to open a PDF file?

PDF can be opened using free programs such as Adobe Acrobat Reader, as well as in most modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Edge, etc.). On mobile devices, PDF is opened through built-in or third-party applications.

Can PDFs be edited?

Yes, PDF files can be edited, but this often requires specialized software (such as Adobe Acrobat Pro) or online services. However, not all PDF files can be edited, especially if they are protected or scanned as images.
